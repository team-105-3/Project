Our Project


SmartCal is tool, made by and for students, to put an end to wasting time. SmartCal automatically looks for gaps in your busy class and work schedule to help you find the time to get things done.


How it Works


    1. Create a free account

    Simply enter your name and email address to get started.
    2. Enter your Events

    Events are things that are fixed into place in your schedule. Classes, work, important meetings, etc.
    3. Enter your Projects

    Projects are things that need to get done, such as homework, essays, studying, etc.
    4. Let SmartCal do the rest

    Our software will work around your schedule to intelligently plan out your projects. When and how long you need to work on each item is automatically planned for you, giving you more time to work, and getting rid of the stress of planning.



Privacy and Security


Privacy

We take privacy seriously. All data entered on our website is securely stored and is not shared anywhere. Data entered is for the sole purpose of maintaining your schedule and for contacting you about your schedule (project due dates) and for resetting your password.

Security

All of your data is securely stored using SHA-256 encryption, and is transmitted via SSL certified connections.
